6th Annual LAMP All Sport Charity Golf Tournament

The Tournament

Each year, the LAMP Athletic Department hosts a charity fundraiser golf tournament. Open to all participants, this event is aimed at helping out approximately 50% of LAMP students who participate in one of the 17 competitive athletic teams while maintaining a high standard. Along with being ranked highly in academics (#1 national magnet, #1 national high school, and #1 state high school), LAMP students pursue their athletic endeavors with excellence. Wether you want to play as an individual or team, or just watch from the sidelines, please considering supporting this charity event.

The Venue

The tournament will be held on October 7th, 2013 at the Arrowhead Country Club on Atlanta Highway in Montgomery. Food and drinks will be available free of charge. Participants may enter in raffles to win our fabulous prizes.
